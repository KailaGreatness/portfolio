Check out my UX work below.



OBS

The Online Beauty Supply is an app that is an alternative for shopping at beauty supply stores. I was inspired to do this for accessibility because, some stores are not accessibile to people with physical disabilities. This app makes it so beauty supply shopping can be done virtually.

Bloop

Bloop was a group project done to draft the idea of introducing social prescribing to the U.S. Social prescribing is a holistic approach to assist people with mental health concerns with community activities.

BBQ

Black Beauty and Quality (an indivdual project) was inspired by the need to embrace Black beauty within the beauty industry. BBQ is meant to uplift Black women, non-men, and femmes with representation and positive news.

Green Queen

During this UX Design project, I was assigned the task of creating a vegan/vegetarian app. To make things more interesting, I made the decision to make the app dessert themed. All images used were images of guaranteed vegan desserts. Making this app showed veganism in a new light. Users are now aware that veganism can have sweet options also.

All prototypes were created with Figma.
